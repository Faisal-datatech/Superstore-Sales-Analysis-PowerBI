# Super Store Sales Analytics & Forecast Power BI Dashboard

## Overview

This project presents an interactive **Power BI dashboard** developed using the Superstore dataset. It provides comprehensive sales analysis and a **15-day sales forecast** to support business decision-making. The dashboard combines data visualization, DAX calculations, data modeling, and forecasting techniques to deliver meaningful business insights.

## Project Objectives

* Analyze sales and profit performance.
* Monitor key business KPIs.
* Evaluate sales by category, sub-category, region, state, customer segment, payment mode, and ship mode.
* Identify sales trends over time.
* Forecast future sales using historical data.

## Forecasting

The forecasting dashboard uses Power BI's built-in forecasting feature to generate a **15-day sales forecast** based on historical sales data. DAX measures are used to calculate and support key analytical metrics displayed throughout the report.

---

## Business Insights

* California records the highest sales.
* Consumer is the leading customer segment.
* Standard Class is the most frequently used shipping method.
* Cash on Delivery (COD) is the most common payment mode.
* Sales increase during the final months of the year.
* The forecasting dashboard provides short-term sales projections for business planning.
